Jodeci

Jodeci is an American R&B group known for their smooth vocals and influence on 1990s R&B music. Information was found using Discogs.

Forever My Lady (1991)
This album is Jodeci’s debut and introduced their smooth and emotional R&B style. The songs focus on love, relationships, and strong vocal harmonies. It helped establish Jodeci as a major group in 1990s R&B music.
Diary of a Mad Band (1993)
This album shows a more mature and serious side of Jodeci. The music includes powerful ballads and emotional themes about love and heartbreak. It became one of their most successful and influential releases.
The Show, the After Party, the Hotel (1995)
This album blends traditional R&B with hip-hop influences. It has a confident and edgy sound that reflects the group’s growth. The album represents Jodeci at the peak of their popularity.
